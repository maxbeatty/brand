Tagline

Product Lockups

Product lockups are set in our corporate typeface, Clan. They do not include the logotype or badge.

Partnerships

B2B Lockups

Design System

Our brand is comprised of many colors, graphics, and icons. Learn how to take full advantage of our design system to brand your experiences.

Color Palette

MaxBeatty Black, White, and Blue are the primary brand colors. Additional tints of each primary color can be used for more complex graphics.

Grid

The grid can be used as a background element in branded designs.

The Dot

The dot is used to highlight a single piece of information. Dots can be placed on top of the grid or city maps.

The Line

The line represents the MaxBeatty rider experience, from pickup to destination.

Badge

The MaxBeatty badge is a secondary mark and should not be used with the MaxBeatty logotype.

City Maps

Learn how to use city maps as a background element in branded designs.

MaxBeatty 100 Maps

These maps are from the MaxBeatty 100 microsite that displayed local ride traffic from one day.

Typography

MaxBeatty’s primary typeface is Clan Pro. It is used across all print, digital, and video creative. Learn how to use this font family.

Font

MaxBeatty’s primary typeface is Clan Pro. It should be used every time it is available.

Hierarchy

Creating hierarchy within typography is key for emphasizing the most important messages.

Video

Learn how to incorporate MaxBeatty branding in videography and motion graphics. More guidelines will be added here soon.

End Slate

Use the animated MaxBeatty logo at the end of a video. Do not combine any CTAs or taglines with this animation.

Brand
Guidelines

The MaxBeatty brand is more than a name. It’s a set of values, attributes, and artwork that reflects the spirit of our company. Using it consistently will reinforce our passion and commitment to providing a world class experience.
